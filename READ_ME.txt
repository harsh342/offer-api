Assumptions

1. We have list of pre-registered merchants who can create offer for themselves. 
	I am using and ENUM to hold the list of merchants. 
2. Application supports Offer Type as CASH_BACK, DISCOUNT_PERCENTAGE,REWARD_POINTS.
3. Application Supports Currency GBP, PERCENT, MERCHANT_POINTS.
4. Percentage offer type can not have Offer Amount greater than 100.
